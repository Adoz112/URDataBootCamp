Data Visualizaton Project Summary

The data visualization project was initially created in order to determine trends in city latitude as it is compared with windspeed, cloudiness, maximum temperature and humdity. The data showed that there were not trends related to windspeed, humidity nor cloudiness, but when the latitude went down, maximum temperature was the only factor that may have been impacted.
